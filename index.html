HOW TO MAKE HOMEMADE WEIGHTS

Want to get stronger but dont feel like paying for a gym membership? Well why not make some at home?

Sand bottles

Source:WikiHow

One way to make small weights is to put sand inside empty water bottles.Little bits of sand is light but there is a bunch of sand, it get heavier.

Milk Weights

Thegoodmama>

Another way to build weights at home is to to find things heavy that would work as a weight.One example is using jugs of milk or water as dumbbells. The bigger the jug, the heavier it would be.

Heavy tire lifting

Source: Facebook

If you want to lift heavier, you can lift big tires because they are heavy so you can practice lifting a single heavy object.
